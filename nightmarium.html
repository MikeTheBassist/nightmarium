Nightmarium

Nightmarium is a game with a simple goal: get to the door. Every level contains multiple enemy spawn points which will continually spill out weird octopus/demon/robot things. Rack up kills for experience points which you can turn in for a boost to your health or the amount of shots you can have on the screen at one time.

Controls: WASD to move, arrow keys to shoot Press space to pause Shoot a wall five times to destroy it
